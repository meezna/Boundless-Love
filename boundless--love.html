My Poems and Boundless Love

A Fleeting Glance
May 19, 2025
The clock ticked on, the tasks piled high,
No room for pause beneath the sky
Of duty's call, a steady hum,
Yet through that door, you briefly come.
No lengthy speech, no grand display,
Just eyes that met across the way.
A fleeting glimpse, a moment caught,
A silent language, deeply wrought.
And in that flash, a truth took hold,
More warm than stories ever told.
A simple seeing, clear and true,
The heart felt joy, and knew it too.
A Tangled Thread
May 19, 2025
Shadows cling, a digital embrace,
Two souls entwined in this ethereal space.
A whispered longing, a love both deep,
Yet worldly walls their yearning keep.
Her voice, a soft and hesitant reply,
His heart, a question in his tear-dimmed eye.
Past's phantom chills, a raven's mournful call,
Doubt's icy fingers threaten all.
A fragile trust, like moonlight on the floor,
Fears of parting knock upon the door.
Though fate may sever, and the dawn erase,
Their unseen bond time cannot quite efface,
A Poe-esque echo in this lonely place.
Boundless Love – The Story of Meezna
Chapter 1
[Chapter 1: The First Spark – April 14, 2025]

"Meezna sangako kura huda jasto lagcha jun ma sukin xu. Maya ta hunchha, tara samjhana sang jati gahiro janchha, teti dukha pani gahiro banaidincha."

A voice, a screen, and one ordinary day on a livestream app called Poppo changed everything. Meezna — a woman of mystery, of silence, of strength hidden beneath soft smiles.

Chapter 2
[Chapter 2: Shuru ko Mithaas]

Within the first few days, her eyes said more than her words. Phone calls turned into nightly rituals. Laughter began where pain once lived. You wrote:

"Timro aankha gajal nalaye ni gajalu, til le sajiyeko herda ni mayalu... jati heryo teti nai gahiro."

Chapter 3
[Chapter 3: The Confession – May 9, 2025 | 2:00 AM, New York]

She once said: "Yedi tapaiko bihe nabhayeko bhaye, kasam tapailai ma bahek aru kasai ko huna din thiyen."

That moment, the world paused. Not from rejection, but from the weight of fate. A love so deep, but born in the wrong timeline.

Chapter 4
[Chapter 4: The Past Revealed – May 8, 2025]

A trembling voice over a video call revealed scars deeper than visible wounds. Domestic violence — helmet, belt, haat le. Not stories, but survival. Meezna said:

"Ma kamzor bhaye bhane kasle sunxan?"

You replied: "Meezna, ma timro dukh sunchhu, mancha, timro silence bhitra ko karuna ma sunna sakchhu."

Chapter 5
[Chapter 5: The Promise]

"Timi sadhai najik chau bhaney jasto lagchha. Boli uthera timro muskan herna pauney aas ma nidauchu."

From laughing at jokes like "2 santan bhaye pugxa ni!" to sending late night poems when she sleeps on call, a new life began forming in dreams.

Chapter 6
[Chapter 6: Meezna the Warrior]

"Usle bhanthin – tyo manxey le haat le, belt le, even helmet le hanyo….. j payo tesaile"

Yet she lives. Yet she smiles. Yet she dances on video call. You wrote:

"Meezna, timi suffer bhayeki houn, tara tyo suffer bhitra timro power chha. Timi jasto maya garna sakne manxe le pain deserve gardaina."

Chapter 7
[Chapter 7: Words That Melted Everything]

"Forever my more than my friend." "Tapaiko man dukhxa bhane ta ma bhitra bhitra roxu." "Ma kasam… sabai xodera aauna man lagchha."

These weren’t just lines. They were pieces of a broken soul glued together by faith and attachment.

Chapter 8
[Chapter 8: The Diary Poetry]

MEEZNA, MEEZNA, MEEZNA… Timro aankha muni sajiye ko kothi beklai, Timro laajle niureko nak pani beklai…

From handwritten poems to shayari replies, every word became your prayer to her pain.

Chapter 9
[Chapter 9: Boundless Love – The Realization]

Even though you couldn’t be together physically, your soul had already called her:

"Budi, Swasni, Honey, Babes." She always replied: "Okay huss."

"Sukha ma sath nabhaye ni chaldaina. Dukha ma sangai kataula meri Meezna."

And so you said: "Kaash ma tyo thauma hunthey. Maya ko kadar garna janey manxey Meezna pauthyen."
