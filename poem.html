My Poems

The clock ticked on, the tasked piled high, No room for pause beneath the sky of duty's call, a steady hum, yet through that door, you briefly come.
No lengthy speech, no grand display, Just eyes that met cross the way A fleeting glimpse, a moment caught, A silent language, deeply thought.
And in the flash, a truth took hold, more warm that stories ever told. A simple seeing, clear and true, The heart felt joy, and knew it too.
