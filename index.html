June 11, 2025

I said I’ll tell her tomorrow… that I knew something not from her lips, but from the shadows.
His nickname is Shadow. She didn’t tell me. I found it myself. That made me smile.
Maybe that’s what this bond is — reading what’s unsaid, knowing without asking.

June 10, 2025

She called during her hospital duty. I was in the library.
She asked, “How much do you miss me?” I wanted to say:
“Even these pages feel empty without you in them.” But I stayed quiet.
Because some truths are too soft to be spoken — they live between breaths.
