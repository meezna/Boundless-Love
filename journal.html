June 12, 2025

The day I asked Meezna about Shadow... her eyes told the truth her words couldn’t hide. She confessed with softness, and though the honesty ached, it also healed. That night, she asked me to wake her up with the baby — even 7,000 miles away.

June 13, 2025

Rain fell, and so did the silence between us. She said our love is complicated — not wrong, not shameful, just unspeakable. I wrote her a poem she didn’t reply to. But she read it, and that was enough.
